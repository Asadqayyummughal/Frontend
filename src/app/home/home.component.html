What does storage insurance cover

Items stored in an affixed storage unit within a self storage or mini storage facility are being stored conventionally. Extra Space, Public Storage, CubeSmart, and Life Storage are all examples of conventional self storage facilities. Items stored in a mobile/portable storage container regardless of container location are being stored in a mobile/ portable storage unit. Mobile Mini, UNITS, MI-BOX, PODS, GO MINIS, and PAK-Rat containers are all examples of portable storage units. Burglary coverage is available for items stored in both conventional and mobile/portable storage units.
